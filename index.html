Scroll box cards in css.

A super simple demo of a mobile scroll box, similar in style to the google news ui on mobile devices.

To see it working best, view on a mobile device.

Code:

The css can be found in scroll-box.css, with a break down of what the main parts are.

The HTML is made of three main elements, container, scroll box and items: 

    <div class="scroll-box-container">
        <div class="scroll-box-scroll">
            <div class="scroll-box-item">
                ...
            </div>
            <div class="scroll-box-item">
                ...
            </div>
        </div>
    </div>
